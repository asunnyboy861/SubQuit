## Description

Stop losing money on subscriptions you don't use!

SubQuit is your simple subscription cancellation assistant. We help you track, manage, and cancel unwanted subscriptions with confidence.

📊 TRACK ALL SUBSCRIPTIONS
Keep every subscription in one place. See your monthly and yearly spending at a glance. Never lose track of what you're paying for.

❌ STEP-BY-STEP CANCELLATION GUIDES
Confused about how to cancel? Our detailed guides walk you through the exact steps to cancel Netflix, Spotify, Adobe, gym memberships, and 50+ popular services. We tell you exactly what to click.

💰 SAVINGS DASHBOARD
See how much you've saved by cancelling unused subscriptions. Visual charts show your spending breakdown by category and your savings over time.

🔔 BILLING REMINDERS
Get notified before your subscriptions renew so you can decide whether to keep or cancel them. No more surprise charges.

☁️ iCLOUD SYNC
Your data syncs seamlessly across all your Apple devices. Your subscription info is always up to date, everywhere.

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, track, or share your personal information. Period.

PREMIUM FEATURES:
• Unlimited cancellation guides
• Full savings dashboard with charts
• iCloud sync across devices
• Priority billing reminders
• New guides added regularly

Download SubQuit today and start saving money on subscriptions you no longer need!
