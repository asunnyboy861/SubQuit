Frequently Asked Questions

Does SubQuit cancel subscriptions for me?

No. SubQuit provides step-by-step cancellation guides to help you cancel subscriptions yourself. We do not have access to your accounts with other services and cannot cancel on your behalf.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your subscription data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync, data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account. We never have access to your data.

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore any previous Premium subscription associated with your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my SubQuit Premium subscription?

SubQuit Premium is managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > SubQuit > Cancel Subscription.

Are cancellation guides always up to date?

We regularly verify and update our guides, but service providers may change their cancellation processes at any time. Always verify the steps directly with the service provider.

How does iCloud Sync work?

When enabled, your subscription data syncs across all devices signed into the same Apple ID via iCloud. Data is end-to-end encrypted.

Refund Policy

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. Refund requests should be directed to Apple:

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the purchase and select "Request a refund"
